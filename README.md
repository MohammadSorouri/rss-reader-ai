## ✨ Key Features

### 1. 📊 3-Tier Semantic Summaries (Powered by GPT-OSS-120b)
No more reading raw web pages. The bot processes incoming feeds on the fly and generates structured summaries containing:
*   **The One-Line Hook:** A single, high-impact headline.
*   **The Digest:** Bulleted high-value takeaways.
*   **The Core Metrics:** Extracted stats, figures, and indicators.

### 2. 🎧 3-Minute Daily Audio Briefing
Wake up, press play, and listen to your news. The bot compiles your unread feeds into a natural, high-fidelity morning podcast using **Edge-TTS** technology. Perfect for your morning commute or breakfast routine.

### 3. 🌐 Seamless Multi-Language Support
Subscribe to feeds in **English, Persian, Spanish, or Russian**. The bot automatically translates, processes, and delivers summaries in your preferred language.

### 4. 🎛️ Clean Dashboard Control
Manage folders, subscriptions, and sync intervals (30 minutes, 2 hours, or manual sync) using an elegant, inline glassmorphic dashboard directly inside Telegram.
