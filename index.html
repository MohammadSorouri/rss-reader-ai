Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about RSS Reader AI

What is RSS Reader AI and how does it work?

RSS Reader AI is an intelligent Telegram bot that converts standard RSS feeds, newsletters, and blogs into concise AI-generated summaries and natural-sounding audio podcasts. It runs in the background and delivers content straight to your Telegram chat.

How do I subscribe to my own custom RSS feeds?

Once you start the bot, open the interactive dashboard menu and click "Feeds". You can manually add any custom RSS, Atom, or Substack URL, or even upload an OPML file to import all your feeds instantly.

How does the text-to-speech (podcast) feature work?

Our backend uses advanced Edge-TTS text-to-speech technology. When a summary digest is generated, the bot converts the text into a clear, human-like audio voice message. You can listen to it like a daily podcast while commuting, working out, or doing chores.

Is RSS Reader AI free to use?

Yes! The Starter Plan is 100% free forever. It allows you to add custom feeds, perform up to 3 AI summaries per day, and listen to standard-quality podcasts. To unlock unlimited summaries, premium voices, and 5-minute sync intervals, you can upgrade to Premium using Telegram Stars.
